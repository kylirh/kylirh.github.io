Mind gone to be forced back.

Eye sight blood.
Mouth taste pain.
Skin feel cold.

Nostril smell emotion.
Ear hear death.

Mind register nothing.
Sense gone out
through experience.

Gone out and soon come back.
Gateway by the Soul.

They are there, together.
As One
coming back.

The Sense through Soul
come back by.

Come back for the cold brood,
darkened through death,
inside home.

Mind register. Please.
Rebirth going forth,
with One come back.

They come back,
mind register One.

Coming back to kill
for salvation,
for glory.

No killer here.
Soon to be.
First to go
now cannot see.

Come bring One back.
Mind is gone, Body reign.
Numbness, death, no pain.

Come back, come back.
Conquer, crush, overpower body.
Dark vanquished by Light.
Mind restored, body is as instrument.

Soul and Sense come back.
