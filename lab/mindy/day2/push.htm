Push

Lift up your head,
Don’t begin to cry.

Pull up your head,
You’re not gonna die.

Push up your head,
You’ve got to try!

Pursue a relentless attack.
Push milestones to a peak!

Go determined into black.
Turn the unbroken cheek.

Beckon it,
Bring them.

Push it.

Conquer them,
Crush it.

When the tide turns to low.
When light battles out bleak.

Relentless against your slow.
Super Giant—push it to a peak!

Look ahead, see straight,
You’re about to fly.

Kick down, see straight,
Only below you rests the ground.

Push up, see straight,
Only stars are left to wait!
