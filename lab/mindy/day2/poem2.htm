The Long Road
I’ve killed myself a thousand times now,
why am I doing it again?
It’s so addicting,
to kill myself.
I think it’ll erase the pain,
make me feel good,
but when I’m still alive afterwards...
...still suffering,
and still in pain,
I ask why?
Why did I do it again?
I’m not going to do it ever again.
I have my word, I promise.
