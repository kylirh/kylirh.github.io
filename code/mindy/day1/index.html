It has been said
and now it is true,
a thousand words have made a picture of you!

But words alone cant describe
the feelings for you I have inside.
And if youre ever stuck at school
just call and Ill give you a ride.

Now roses are red,
violets are blue,
and I love you!

I love your eyes
when you look into mine so deep.
I like your smile
when you chuckle in your sleep.

Im writing this cheesy poem
as fast as I can
before I go and eat some ham.

But I want you to know
that it is of you I am thinking
and of a way to show my feelings
even more than speaking.

So Ive concocted an adventure
to send you upon
more grand than scaling a canyon!

Many times I shall send
a special clue only to you
which will guide and direct
to something beautiful and blue!

What it is and where
youll just have to wait,
but please dont sit on a gate!

Right here you shall see
the location of the first clue.
Its somewhere on campus
waiting just for you!
