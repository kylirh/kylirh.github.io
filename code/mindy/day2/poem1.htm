Or so it seems

The man who reads, also writes.

the man fights, not sure if he’s winning or loosing.

the coach said, "Do your best, and you’ve won."

he fights on, falls and stands.

he’s doing his best, but he can do better.

he doesn’t know why he’s fighting.

But he fights on.

he tells the coach, "How am I doing?"

the man’s winning, but loosing in his mind.

The man who writes, also reads.

 

Or so it all seems.

                        
Is there order in chaos?
