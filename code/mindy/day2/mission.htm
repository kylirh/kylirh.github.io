Mission Statement

Always encourage others' dreams,
but never tell them what to do.
Never cease Changing
and Growing for the Better.
Never Willingly Do Something Easy,
but yet Just Do It.
While Work is Hard,
so is a Balance in Life.
Therefore, always remember
and live by Your Principles First,
for this is the only way to
Develop Integrity and Character.
Live each day of Life
like it were your Last,
never forgetting the
Pursuit of Perfection.
Excellence is a series of Good Habits,
and so are Goals.
So Conquer and Command your
Habits, Time, and Appetites.
Keep in Mind that only through
Choosing to be Happy, Kind,
Well-Rounded, and Disciplined
will you achieve Ultimate Success.

Choose thy fate, mortal... and choose it well.
